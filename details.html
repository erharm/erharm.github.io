Location

We live in Arlington, WA which is about halfway between Seattle and Bellingham, about an hour drive from each. Although our party is on Saturday, we will be around all weekend! Setting up on Friday and Saturday morning, with a Sunday group breakfast for those who camped overnight or are staying nearby. Feel free to stop by anytime throughout the weekend to gather and connect.

Lodging

You are welcome to bring a tent, car or camper and hunker down in our yard on Saturday night!

Convenient Options:

  • Angel in the Winds Casino, located 2 miles down the road and has a hotel, restaurants, bowling and more.
  • Tulalip Casino Resort is a larger and more upscale casino, about 15 minutes south of us located next to the Seattle Outlet Mall.
  • We're located smack between Arlington, WA and Stanwood, WA if you are looking to book local.
  • RV hookups? Angel of the Winds has a new RV Resort one exit north from us on Interstate 5.

Parking

We will have a small amount of parking on site, however, to help accommodate all of our guests we are asking that you please consider meeting other attendees at Angel in the Winds Casino (free parking) and spending an intimate two mile drive over together. We may try to offer a shuttle service between locations, and we will post updates about that as specifics continue to unfold.
